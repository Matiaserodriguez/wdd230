Your weather 24

Target audience

Your weather 24 will impact directly on any kind of people that wanna know the time NOW. People who are going to do outdoor activities need to know NOW if it is going to rain, or what is going to happen during the day. In turn, the people who will go to work must know what the weather will be like, to know what they can wear, and how to organize their day.

Personas

Juan Perez

Juan is 16 years old and after studying in high school, he always sets aside time to do activities during the day. Juan always needs to know what the weather will be like today in order to organize himself.

José Sandoval

José is a father of a family, he is 36 years old and every day he goes to work in the morning and arrives in the afternoon. His work is very flexible in terms of clothing, so he can always go to YOUR WEATHER 24 to find out what to wear, whether to wear a coat or even ride a bike.

Scenarios

Juan plays soccer

Juan is going to play outdoor soccer with his friends after school. They sky is pretty gray and they are not sure if it's going to rain and if they will be able to play soccer. Then Juan remembers that YOUR WEATHER 24 can tell him that, and he opens our page in order to find that info.

José want to ride a bike

José bought a new bike, and he is wondering if the weather during the week is going to be good enough to go to and from work with the bicycle. So remember that YOUR WEATHER 24 has an extended forecast and enter our page in search of that information.

Juan schedules a Picnic

Juan and his friends want to go to a Picnic, but they are wondering if it will be hot or cold. Juan always remembers that YOUR WEATHER 24 has the weather for any specific day, and he looks at his phone and found that info.

José must plan an activity at work

José was told by his supervisor to make an icebreaker activity because there are a lot of new employees in the company. He needs to make a plan for that day. Thanks to YOUR WEATHER 24, he is able to see how the weather of that day will be and planify according to it.

Juan has a science project

Juan must finish an assignment and one of the tasks is to verify the movement of the moon. He needs to verify what specific days at the evening the sky will be clear and cloudless. In order to choose the specific days, he consults to YOUR WEATHER 24.
